# 🥓 Vision & Philosophy: The Organic Standard
**Status:** Canonical :: Lead Architect Approved

## 1. The Core Objective
Professionals are drowning in meeting noise. **Crispy Bacon** exists to build **Time-to-Think**. We don't just "process data"; we transform 5 hours of scattered audio into 5 minutes of high-clarity strategic notes that mirror the cognitive structure of a top-tier analyst.

## 2. The Organic Principles
* **Warmth (Humanist Design):** Interfaces should feel like a premium notebook, not a command terminal. We use warm Sandstone backgrounds, elegant serif headers, and tactile Charcoal/Rust accents. The app is a quiet place for deep thought.
* **Dense (Signal > Noise):** No AI fluff. We banish "In this meeting, the team discussed..." and replace it with direct facts. We prefer objective bullet points and structured decisions over long paragraphs.
* **Natural (Human Language):** We avoid engineering jargon in the UI like "Ingestion," "Telemetry," or "Latency." We use "Import," "Activity," and "Speed." The assistant is a partner, not a utility.

## 3. The No-Bot Standard
We respect the social fabric of professional calls.
* **Direct Capture:** Audio is captured locally via the browser's native `MediaRecorder` or via direct file upload.
* **Invisible Intelligence:** We do not send bot avatars into Zoom or Teams meetings. No intrusive recording warnings, no interruption of the human flow.

## 4. Truth-First Reasoning
Our intelligence engine is honest. If a decision wasn't made, the AI explicitly states "No decision detected." We prioritize **Accuracy over Politeness**.
