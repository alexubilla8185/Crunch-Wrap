# 🌯 Vision & Philosophy: Crunch Wrap
**Status:** Canonical :: Lead Architect Approved

## 1. The Core Objective
Professionals are drowning in messy data and scattered audio. **Crunch Wrap** exists to instantly synthesize chaos. We don't just "process data"; we take hours of unstructured files, *crunch* them down, and *wrap* them up into high-clarity strategic insights in under 3 seconds. 

## 2. The UX/UI Principles (Material 3 Expressive)
* **The "Snap" UX:** We banish progressive loading and trickling data. We use optimistic UI and atomic cache updates to deliver the entire processed payload to the screen in one satisfying "Snap."
* **M3 Expressive Aesthetics:** We utilize Google's Material 3 Expressive design language. We rely on fluid spatial layers, deep OLED/charcoal dark modes (`--surface`), pure white light modes, pill-shaped components, and extreme border-radii (`rounded-3xl`).
* **Subtle Intelligence:** We use soft AI gradients, frosted glassmorphism, and kinetic micro-animations to indicate power, rather than heavy, distracting UI elements.

## 3. The No-Bot Standard
We respect the privacy and social fabric of professional workflows.
* **Direct Local Capture:** Audio is captured natively via the browser and saved locally before touching the cloud.
* **Invisible Intelligence:** We do not send bot avatars into Zoom or Teams meetings. No intrusive recording warnings, no interruption of the human flow. Privacy-first, always.
